Healthy Food v/s Unhealthy Food

  • Healthy food Importance

    Healthy foods are those that provide you with the nutrients you need to sustain your body's well-being and retain energy. Water, carbohydrates, fat, protein, vitamins, and minerals are the key nutrients that make up a healthy, balanced diet.Healthy Food might not be as tasty as junk food but can be very delicious.

  • Effects of Unhealthy food
